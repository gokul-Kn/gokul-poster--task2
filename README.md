# ☕ The Daily Drink

*"Good Day • Good Mood • Good Coffee"*

## About
The Daily Drink is a coffee brand/logo concept designed with a clean, calm, and modern aesthetic. The design combines a coffee cup, steam elements, and natural leaf shapes to create a relaxing and refreshing identity.

This logo represents the idea that a great cup of coffee can improve your mood and make your day better.

## Design Elements
- ☕ Coffee cup – Represents warmth and freshness
- 🌿 Leaf design – Symbolizes natural ingredients and freshness
- ♨️ Steam effect – Shows hot and aromatic coffee
- 🎨 Soft color palette – Creates a peaceful and premium feel

## Brand Message
**Good Day • Good Mood • Good Coffee**
